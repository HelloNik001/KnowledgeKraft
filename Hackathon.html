Hackathon Websites

DevPost

DevPost hosts various hackathons and provides a platform for participants to showcase their projects. Their website features a clean and modern design with vibrant colors and smooth animations.

MLH (Major League Hacking)

MLH organizes hackathons worldwide and their website boasts a sleek and professional design. It utilizes dynamic elements and interactive features to engage users.

HackerEarth

HackerEarth hosts hackathons, coding challenges, and tech recruiting events. Their website features a modern and minimalist design with subtle animations and a focus on usability.

Hackster.io

Hackster.io is a community platform for hardware hackathons and projects. Their website has a visually appealing design with bold colors, clear typography, and engaging visuals.

AngelHack

AngelHack organizes hackathons and provides resources for developers and startups. Their website features a dynamic design with eye-catching graphics, smooth transitions, and a user-friendly layout.
